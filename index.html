About The Game

Western Sniper puts you in the boots of an elite marksman in the untamed Wild West. Face increasingly challenging missions that will test your accuracy, timing, and tactical thinking. With stunning visuals, realistic ballistics, and an immersive Wild West atmosphere, each shot counts as you build your reputation as the most feared sharpshooter in the territory. Unlock new weapons, master different shooting techniques, and prove your skills in this action-packed shooting adventure.
